Sreenath Paleri

PhD Candidate, Atmospheric & Oceanic Sciences

University of Wisconsin - Madison

Hello visitor! I am Sreenath Paleri, a PhD candidate in Prof. Ankur Desai’s Ecometeorology Lab, Department of Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences, University of Wisconsin - Madison. I am interested in measuring and modeling the atmospheric boundary layer to better understand surface-atmospheric exchanges over heterogeneous surfaces.

For my doctoral thesis I am using field measurements collected as part of the CHEESEHEAD19 field experiment and setting up large eddy simulations of the field experiment days to study the role of low frequency, secondary circulations in surface-atmospheric transport. Please feel free to have a look at my current and past work and let me know if you have any questions or would like to discuss any of the ideas further!

You can reach me at paleri@wisc.edu or send me direct message on any of my linked socials.
